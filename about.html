About La Familia Chambers

Empowering businesses and fostering economic growth in Nairobi and Mlolongo through collaboration, advocacy, and innovation.

Our Mission

To advocate for a favorable business environment, provide valuable resources, and foster a strong network among businesses in Nairobi and Mlolongo, enabling them to thrive, innovate, and contribute to sustainable economic development.

Our Vision

To be the leading voice and catalyst for business prosperity in the region, recognized for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and community empowerment.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our endeavors.
  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships and teamwork to achieve shared goals.
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas and technologies to drive progress.
  • Excellence: Striving for superior quality and impact in our services and initiatives.
  • Community: Building a supportive and inclusive environment for all members.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of visionary entrepreneurs, La Familia Chambers began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a unified platform where local businesses could connect, share insights, and collectively address challenges. What started as a small gathering has grown into a vibrant community of hundreds of members, actively shaping the economic landscape of Nairobi and Mlolongo.

Over the years, we have successfully advocated for policies supporting local businesses, organized numerous impactful events, and provided essential training and networking opportunities. Our journey is a testament to the power of collective action and the unwavering spirit of entrepreneurship in Kenya.
