Key Business Sectors in Nairobi

Technology & Innovation

Nairobi, often dubbed 'Silicon Savannah', is a thriving tech hub with numerous startups, incubators, and established IT firms driving digital transformation across Africa.

View Tech Businesses

Finance & Banking

As East Africa's financial capital, Nairobi hosts major banks, investment firms, and financial institutions, serving as a gateway for regional and international finance.

View Finance Businesses

Tourism & Hospitality

From safari adventures to luxury hotels, Nairobi's tourism sector is booming, attracting visitors globally and offering a rich blend of culture, wildlife, and urban experiences.

View Tourism Businesses

Manufacturing & Industry

A growing manufacturing base contributes significantly to Nairobi's economy, producing goods for local consumption and export, from textiles to processed foods.

Retail & Commerce

Nairobi's bustling markets and modern shopping malls showcase a dynamic retail sector, offering everything from local crafts to international brands.

Agriculture & Agribusiness

Surrounded by fertile lands, Nairobi serves as a key hub for agribusiness, connecting farmers with markets and driving innovation in agricultural practices.
