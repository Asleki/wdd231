Our Comprehensive Services

General Medical Consultations

Our experienced general practitioners provide thorough examinations, diagnoses, and treatment plans for a wide range of common illnesses and health concerns.

Payment Methods & Pricing:

  • Cash/M-Pesa: KES 1,500 - KES 2,500 (depending on complexity)
  • Major Medical Insurances: NHIF, Britam, Jubilee, AAR (Co-pay may apply)
  • Card Payments: Visa, MasterCard

Emergency Ambulance Services

Equipped with state-of-the-art medical equipment, our ambulances provide rapid response and safe transportation for critical emergencies, staffed by trained paramedics.

Payment Methods & Pricing:

  • Emergency Call Out: KES 8,000 - KES 20,000 (within Mlolongo/Nairobi, depending on distance and severity)
  • Inter-county Transfer: Quoted per km
  • Direct Billing to Insurance: Available for approved partners

Diagnostic Laboratory Services

We offer a full range of accurate and timely laboratory tests including blood work, urine analysis, pathology, and more, crucial for precise diagnoses.

Payment Methods & Pricing:

  • Full Blood Count (FBC): KES 700
  • Urinalysis: KES 500
  • Malaria Test: KES 600
  • Comprehensive Metabolic Panel: KES 2,000
  • Insurance Coverage: Check with your provider for specific lab test coverage.

On-site Pharmacy

Our well-stocked pharmacy provides convenient access to prescribed medications and over-the-counter drugs, ensuring you get the right treatment without delay.

Payment Methods & Pricing:

  • Prescription Medications: Prices vary by medication (regulated by PPB)
  • Over-the-Counter: Standard retail pricing
  • Payment Options: Cash, M-Pesa, Card, Insurance (for covered prescriptions)

Specialized Pediatric Care

Dedicated medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on their unique health and developmental needs, from routine check-ups to vaccinations.

Payment Methods & Pricing:

  • Pediatric Consultation: KES 2,000 - KES 3,500
  • Vaccinations: Prices vary by vaccine (e.g., Polio, Measles)
  • Child Health Check-ups: KES 1,800
  • Accepted Insurances: AAR, Jubilee, Sanlam (verify child-specific benefits)

Mortuary & Funeral Support Services

In times of loss, AfyaCare Clinic extends compassionate support, offering mortuary services and guidance through the necessary arrangements with dignity and respect.

Payment Methods & Pricing:

  • Mortuary Storage (per day): KES 500 - KES 1,000
  • Embalming Services: KES 10,000 - KES 15,000
  • Basic Funeral Arrangement Support: KES 5,000 (consultation & coordination)
  • Payment Options: Cash, Bank Transfer, M-Pesa

Meet Our Professionals & Our Journey

At AfyaCare Clinic, we believe in a holistic approach to health, driven by a team of dedicated and compassionate professionals. Our journey began with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality medical care to every member of our community.

Dr. Almasi Juma

Chief Executive Officer

With over 20 years in healthcare management, Dr. Juma leads AfyaCare Clinic with a passion for community health and innovative medical solutions. His vision guides our commitment to excellence and patient-centered care.

Dr. Zawadi Mwai

Lead General Practitioner

Dr. Mwai is a highly respected GP with 15 years of experience. Her expertise spans family medicine, chronic disease management, and preventative health. Patients value her empathetic approach and thorough diagnostics.

Dr. Kiprono Cheruiyot

Pediatrician

Specializing in child health, Dr. Cheruiyot is beloved by families for his gentle demeanor and expert care for children of all ages. He focuses on healthy development and age-appropriate medical interventions.
