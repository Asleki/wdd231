Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about La Familia Chambers, membership, events, and services.

Last Updated: July 11, 2025

General Questions

What is La Familia Chambers?

La Familia Chambers is a dynamic business association dedicated to fostering economic growth, promoting local businesses, and building a strong, interconnected community in Nairobi and its environs. We provide networking opportunities, resources, advocacy, and support to our members.

Who can join La Familia Chambers?

Membership is open to businesses of all sizes, entrepreneurs, and professionals operating within Nairobi and the surrounding regions. Whether you're a startup, a small business, or a large corporation, we welcome you to join our growing family.

What are the benefits of membership?

Membership offers a wide range of benefits, including:

  • Networking opportunities with other local businesses.
  • Access to exclusive workshops, seminars, and training.
  • Advocacy and representation on business issues.
  • Marketing and promotional opportunities through our directory and events.
  • Access to business resources and mentorship programs.
  • Discounts on services from fellow members.

Membership Questions

How do I become a member?

You can become a member by visiting our Join La Familia page, filling out the online application form, and submitting the required membership fees. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you shortly.

What are the membership fees?

Membership fees vary based on the type and size of your business. Please refer to the detailed breakdown on our Join La Familia page or contact our membership department for specific pricing.

How do I update my member profile or business listing?

Members can log in to their dedicated member portal to update their business information, contact details, and service offerings. If you require assistance, please contact our support team.

Events & Programs

How do I register for an event?

You can view all upcoming events on our Events Calendar. Click on the event you're interested in, and follow the registration instructions provided on the event details page.

Can non-members attend events?

Some of our events are open to non-members, often at a different registration fee. Member-exclusive events will be clearly indicated. We encourage non-members to attend open events to experience the value of La Familia Chambers.

How can I propose an event or workshop?

We welcome suggestions for new events and workshops that benefit our community. Please reach out to our events coordination team via email with your proposal, and we'll review it.

Website & Technical Questions

How do I search the Member Directory?

Visit our Member Directory page. You can use the search bar to look for specific business names or keywords, and apply filters by category, location, and other criteria to narrow down your search results.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We implement various technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. For more details, please refer to our comprehensive Privacy Policy.

I'm having trouble logging in, what should I do?

If you're experiencing login issues, please try the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team at support@lafamiliachambers.co.ke for assistance.
