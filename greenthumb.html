About Green Thumb Landscaping

Green Thumb Landscaping has been transforming outdoor spaces across Nairobi and its environs since 2018. Our passion lies in creating breathtaking, sustainable landscapes that not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of your property but also provide serene and functional environments for relaxation and enjoyment.

We believe a well-designed and maintained garden is an extension of your living or working space, contributing to well-being and increasing property value. Our team of certified horticulturists and experienced landscapers works closely with clients to bring their vision to life, ensuring every project, big or small, is executed with precision and care.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of landscaping and garden maintenance services tailored to meet the unique needs of residential and commercial clients:

  • Landscape Design & Installation: From conceptualization to execution, including hardscaping (patios, walkways) and softscaping (planting, lawns).
  • Garden Maintenance: Regular upkeep services including mowing, pruning, weeding, fertilization, and pest control.
  • Irrigation Systems: Design and installation of efficient watering systems to ensure your garden thrives with minimal effort.
  • Tree & Shrub Care: Expert care for trees and shrubs, including planting, pruning, and health management.
  • Vertical Gardens & Green Walls: Innovative solutions for urban spaces, bringing greenery to vertical surfaces.
  • Water Features: Design and installation of ponds, fountains, and other water elements to add tranquility.
  • Seasonal Planting: Advice and implementation for seasonal flowers and plants to keep your garden vibrant year-round.
