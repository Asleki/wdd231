Our Culinary Journey

At Taste of Africa Catering, we bring the vibrant flavors and rich culinary traditions of Africa and beyond directly to your event. Founded with a passion for authentic cuisine and impeccable service, we specialize in creating unforgettable dining experiences tailored to your needs.

From intimate family gatherings to grand corporate events, our expert chefs craft diverse menus using the freshest local ingredients, ensuring every bite is a celebration of taste and culture. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, presentation, and making your event truly special.

Signature Dishes & Menu Highlights

Authentic Kenyan Pilau

Fragrant rice dish cooked with tender beef or chicken, aromatic spices, and fresh herbs, a true East African classic.

Soft Layered Chapatis

Hand-rolled, flaky flatbreads perfect for scooping up curries or as a delightful snack on their own.

Matoke (Green Banana Stew)

A comforting Ugandan stew of green bananas cooked with tender meat, vegetables, and rich peanut sauce.

Spicy Jollof Rice

The iconic West African rice dish, richly flavored with tomatoes, peppers, and savory spices, available with chicken or fish.

Crispy Vegetarian Samosas

Golden fried pastry pockets filled with spiced potatoes and peas, a perfect appetizer.

Kuku Paka (Coconut Chicken Curry)

A creamy and flavorful coastal Kenyan chicken curry, slow-cooked in coconut milk with traditional spices.
